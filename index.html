About me.

How I Got Here

I first played with code when I was 7 years old after dear old dad bought me a Commodore 64 from an old man down the street. A box with hundreds of games on big flimsy black floppy disks came with it so, needless to say, I spent more time with it than anything else. I grew to love coding, consistantly returning to it throughout my life.


While spending most of last year keeping safe inside, I realized I might be able to shift my career away from IT and into the world of software development. I spent thousands of hours studying from books, articles, and online communities, completing quite a few instructor-led courses offered online. I explored anything and everything interesting, completing dozens of course projects and some of my own for multiple platforms including embedded, desktop, mobile, games, and web development. I also studied clean code, test driven development, design patterns, refactoring, and software construction.
