About me.

How I Got Here

I first played with code when I was 7 years old after dear old dad bought me a used Commodore 64. A box with hundreds of games on big flimsy floppy disks came with it so, needless to say, I spent more time with it than anything else. I grew to love coding, consistently returning to it throughout my life.


While spending most of last year keeping safe inside with extra time to think, I realized an opportunity to shift my career trajectory away from purely IT and into the world of software development. I spent thousands of hours refreshing previous skills and gaining additional knowledge and abilities, studying from books, articles, documentation, online communities and instructor-led courses offered online.


Now, though I program mostly for the web, I also have some experience developing for android, desktop, game engines, and embedded systems, where I've used other popular languages, libraries, frameworks, and tools.
